## Arithmetic
Addition: <br> $a+b$

Subtraction: <br> $a-b$

Multiplication: <br> $a \cdot b$, <br> $a \times b$, <br> $a \ast b$

Division: <br> $a \colon b$, <br> $a / b$, <br> $a \div b$, <br> $\frac{a}{b}$

Modular Division: <br> $5\mod 2=1$

Plus or Minus: <br> $\pm a$

Nth power: <br> $a^n$

Nth root: <br> $\sqrt[n]{a}$


## Equality
Equals: <br> $3=1+2$

Not Equal: <br> $3\neq4$

Equivalent: <br> $a\equiv b$

Proportional: <br> $a\propto b$

Approximately: <br> $\sin(0.01)\approx 0.01$

## Comparison
Less than: <br> $a<b$

Greater than: <br> $a>b$

Less than or equal to: <br> $a \leq b$

Greate than or equal to: <br> $a \geq b$

Much less than: <br> $a \ll b$

Much greater than: <br> $a \gg b$

## Algebra

Facrotial: <br> $5!=5 \times 4 \times 3 \times 2 \times 1$

Absolute Value: <br> $|-5|=5$

Function: <br> $f(x)=2x^2$

Change: <br> $\Delta x = x_1 - x_0$

Pi: <br> $\pi = = 3.14159...$

Euler's Constant: <br> $e = 2.71828

Summation: <br> $\displaystyle\sum_{k=3}^5k^2=3^2 + 4^2 + 5^2 = 50$

Series Product: <br> $\displaystyle\prod_{x=2}^4 x^2 = 2^2 \times 3^2 \times 4^2 = 576$

Brackets & Parentheses: <br> $[\ldots] (\ldots)$

## Angles

Angle: <br> $\angle$

Degree, Arc, Min, Arc Sec: <br> $30\degree$, <br> $45\rq$, <br> $30\rq\rq$

Radians: <br> $360\degree = 2\pi rad$

## Probability & Statistics

Probability of A: <br> $P(A)$ or $\Pr(A)$

Intersection Probability of A and B: <br> $P(A \cap B)$

Union Probability of A or B: <br> $P(A \cup B)$

Conditional Probability of A, given B: <br> $P(A|B)$

Median: <br> $\tilde{x}$

Population Mean: <br> $\mu$, <br> $\overline{x}$, <br> $\langle x \rangle$

Standard Deviation: <br> $\sigma$

Variance: <br> $\sigma^2$

## Linear Algebra

Vectors: <br> $\mathbf{v}\overline{v}\vec{v}$

Row Vector: <br> $v=\begin{pmatrix}1&2&3\end{pmatrix}$

Column Vector: <br> $w=\begin{pmatrix}4\cr5\cr6\cr\end{pmatrix}$

Dot Product: <br>$\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{w}$, <br> $(v,w)$, <br> $\left<v | w\right>$

Cross Product: <br> $v \times w$

Length of v: <br> $|v|$

Norm of v: <br> $||v||$

### Matrices

Matrix 2x3: <br> $A=\begin{bmatrix}1&2&3\cr4&5&6\end{bmatrix}$

Product: <br> $A \cdot B$

Hadamard Product: <br> $A \circ B$

Kronecker Product: <br> $A \otimes B$

Transposed Matrix: <br> $A^T$

Hermitian Matrix or Conjuate Transpose: <br> $A^\dag$, <br> $A^\ast$

Inverse Matrix: <br> $A^{-1}$

Determinant: $|A|$

Norm: $||A||$

## Calculus

Example Function: <br> $y = \frac{x^2}{4}$

Integration: <br> $A = \int_1^4\frac{x^2}{x} dx$

First Derivative With Respect to $x$: <br> $\frac{df}{dx}$

Partial Derivative with Respect to $x$: <br> $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$

First and Second Derivative of Function: <br> $f\rq$ <br> $f\rq\rq$

First and Second Derivative With Respect to Time: <br> $\dot f$ <br> $\ddot f$

## Complex Numbers

Imaginary Unit $i$: <br> $z=3+2i$

Real Part of Complex Number: <br> $\Re(z)=3$ <br> $\operatorname{Re} (z)=3$

Imaginary Part of Complex Number: <br> $\Im(z)=2$ <br> $\operatorname{Im} (z) = 2$

Complex Conjugate: <br> $\bar{z}=z^*=3-2i$
